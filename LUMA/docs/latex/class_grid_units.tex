Velocity in lattice units to velocity in physical units. 

Converts velocity component from lattice units to m/s. It uses the L\+\_\+\+U\+\_\+phys introduced by the user, dx and dt. You can introduce any L\+\_\+\+U\+\_\+phys you want, but the reference lenght (usualy the width of the domain) , the Re number and the L\+BM parameters will remain the same. So you will be implicitly changing the physical viscosity of your fluid when you change L\+\_\+\+U\+\_\+phys
